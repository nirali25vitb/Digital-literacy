# Social Media Use Checklist

## Do’s
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- Think before posting (consider long-term impact)  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone  
- Protect your personal information and review privacy settings regularly  



## Don’ts
- Don’t share passwords or OTPs with anyone  
- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information  
- Don’t overshare personal details (address, phone number, etc.)  
- Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
