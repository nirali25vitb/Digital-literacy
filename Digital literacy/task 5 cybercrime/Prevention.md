# Prevention



### Prevention Tips

- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication  
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages  
- Always verify the sender before responding to urgent or alarming messages  
- Keep your devices updated with the latest software and security patches  
- Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi  
- Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store  
- Regularly review privacy settings on social media platforms  

### UPI / Financial Safety Tips

- Never approve unknown UPI collect requests or payment requests  
- Double-check UPI IDs and recipient names before sending money  



### Report Cybercrime

- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in
